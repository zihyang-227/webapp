Overview of SP Builder

This user tutorial aims to help you understand how to use our SP Builder to build an experiment from scratch.
We suggest opening a task builder tab to follow our guide step-by-step.
We will teach you how to:
1. Set up your stimuli pool
2. Prime your stimuli pool
3. Design your trial flow
4. Load/Save your design
5. Analyze your data
